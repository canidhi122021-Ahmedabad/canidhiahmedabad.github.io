Frequently Asked Questions

Can I file ITR if I missed the due date?
Yes, you can file a Belated Return u/s 139(4) by 31st December of the Assessment Year, typically with a penalty.
What is the difference between Financial Year (FY) and Assessment Year (AY)?
FY is the year in which income is earned (e.g., 2024-25). AY is the following year in which that income is assessed and returns are filed (e.g., 2025-26).
Do I need to file ITR if my income is below â‚¹ 3 Lakhs?
Mandatory filing is not required, but filing a "Nil Return" is recommended for loan processing and visa applications.
What documents are required for GST Registration?
1. PAN & Aadhaar. 2. Electricity bill/Rent Agreement. 3. Bank Statement/Cancelled Cheque. 4. Photo.

International & NRI Taxation

TDS on NRI Payments (Sec 195)

Any payment to a Non-Resident (Interest, Capital Gains, Royalty) is subject to TDS. Rates vary as per DTAA or Income Tax Act (whichever is beneficial).

  • Interest: 20% + Cess.
  • LTCG (Property): 20% + Cess.

Foreign Remittance (Form 15CA/CB)

Banks require these forms before transferring money abroad.

  • 15CA: Self-declaration by remitter.
  • 15CB: Certificate from Chartered Accountant confirming tax payment.
